FAQ:

  • How can I add a conference to PhD mate?
    You can't add a conference directly to PhD mate. We get our information from wikicfp.com. Therefore if you announce your conference there it will show up on PhD mate.
  • Why are some conferences/journals located in the middle of the Atlantic ocean?
    Because Google Geocode API couldn't resolve their location :)
  • How fresh are the results?
    The information on this page is updated daily. If you need hourly updates go to wikicfp.com.
  • How complete are the results?
    wikicfp.com has a very large dataset of conferences and journals but it does not have every conference/journal. We are only as complete as they are :)
  • How accurate are the results?
    As accurate as we could make them. Locations may not always be accurate and we take no responsibility for the data provided.
  • Can I rely on the information on this page 100%?
    NO. We have tried our best to provide PhD students (us included) with a useful tool. We take absolutely no responsibility for what may happen as a result of anyone using this service. This service is provided on an as is basis.
  • What is the meaning of the different colors?
    The different colors indicate the tier information for each conference.
    • Top-level conferences get a green color.
    • Tier 2 conferences are displayed with orange markers.
    • Third-level conferences have a yellow marker.
    • Conferences without tier information, but with ranking are colored in blue.
    • Conferences with neither tier nor ranking information get a marker in pink.
