• Why is conference X or Y not listed? Please keep in mind that CFP Explorer ONLY lists conferences which have and active Call For Papers (CFP)
    Reason # 1: There are no active Call For Papers (CFP with a submission deadline still in the future) from that conference.
    Reason # 2: The CFP is not listed (or known) by the sources we crawl.
  • How can I add a conference to CFP Explorer? You can't add a conference directly to CFP Explorer. We get our information from wikicfp.com. Therefore if you announce your conference there it will show up on CFP Explorer.
  • How fresh are the results? The information on this page is updated daily. If you need hourly updates go to wikicfp.com.
  • How complete are the results? wikicfp.com has a very large dataset of conferences and journals but it does not have every conference/journal. We are only as complete as they are :)
  • How accurate are the results? As accurate as we could make them. Locations may not always be accurate and we take no responsibility for the data provided.
  • Can I rely on the information on this page 100%? NO. We have tried our best to provide PhD students (us included) with a useful tool. We take absolutely no responsibility for what may happen as a result of anyone using this service. This service is provided on an as is basis.
